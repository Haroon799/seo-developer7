Inside Our Strategy & Tools

A transparent look at exactly how we approach SEO and Local SEO — the strategy behind every decision, and the industry-standard tools we use to research, optimize, and track results for real client projects.

Client & Partner Meeting

Hi-Tech Engineering Works

Industrial & Engineering Services
Goal: Improve organic rankings for engineering search terms

Strategy

  • Keyword ResearchTargeted high-intent, industry-specific terms that match real buyer searches rather than generic, oversaturated keywords.
  • Technical SEO AuditChecked site speed, mobile responsiveness, and crawlability, fixing broken links and indexing issues.
  • On-Page OptimizationRewrote titles, meta descriptions, and headers to align with target keywords while staying natural to read.
  • Content StructuringGave each major service its own optimized page instead of one generic page competing for too many terms.
  • Backlink & Authority BuildingBuilt relevant, industry-appropriate backlinks to strengthen domain authority and trust.
  • Ongoing MonitoringTracked keyword movement and adjusted strategy based on what was gaining or losing traction.

Tools Used

  • Keyword researchKeyword Planner, Ahrefs/SEMrush
  • Technical SEO auditScreaming Frog, Search Console
  • Site speed & performancePageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix
  • Rank trackingSEMrush/Ahrefs Rank Tracker
  • Traffic monitoringGoogle Analytics (GA4)
  • On-page SEO scoringSurfer SEO / Yoast

Zayn Beauty

Beauty & Personal Care
Goal: Improve local discovery & nearby search visibility

Strategy

  • Local Keyword ResearchTargeted "near me" and location-based terms specific to the beauty industry.
  • Google Business Profile OptimizationFully optimized business info, categories, photos, and posts for Maps and the local "3-Pack."
  • Citation Building & NAP ConsistencyKept Name, Address, and Phone consistent across directories to build local trust signals.
  • Review Generation StrategyEncouraged satisfied customers to leave reviews, paired with prompt, professional responses.
  • On-Page + Local ContentCreated location-specific landing pages addressing common local search queries.
  • Social Proof IntegrationLinked social media activity back to the profile to reinforce local relevance signals.

Tools Used

  • Local keyword researchKeyword Planner, BrightLocal
  • Profile managementGoogle Business Profile
  • Local rank trackingBrightLocal, Local Falcon
  • Citation & NAP consistencyBrightLocal / Moz Local
  • Review monitoringGoogle Business Profile
  • On-page SEOSEMrush / Ahrefs
