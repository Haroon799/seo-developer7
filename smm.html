Social Media FAQ

Social Media Management FAQs

Straightforward answers on what social media management involves, what results you can expect, and why consistency matters.

4.8 billion people use social media globally. Your audience is already there — the question is whether your brand shows up consistently and professionally. Without active management, you miss daily opportunities to build trust, reach new audiences, and convert followers into paying customers.
It depends on your audience. Instagram & Facebook suit most B2C businesses. LinkedIn is essential for B2B. YouTube Shorts & Reels drive the highest organic reach right now. We audit your audience, competitors and goals to recommend the right platform mix — not every platform, just the right ones.
Inconsistency is the #1 reason social media fails for businesses. Algorithms penalise irregular posting, follower trust erodes, and brand recall drops. A dormant social profile actively damages credibility — visitors assume the business is inactive or struggling.
Our SMM service covers: content strategy & calendar, graphic design & copywriting, post scheduling, community management (comments & DMs), hashtag research, monthly analytics reports, and platform optimisation. Reels/video content is available as an add-on.
Our standard packages include 3–5 posts per week depending on the plan. Each post is strategically planned — educational, promotional, engagement, and brand posts are balanced to keep audiences interested without feeling sold to constantly.
